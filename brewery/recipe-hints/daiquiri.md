# 🍓 Daiquiri

> A daiquiri is a cocktail most commonly made with rum with a citrus juice and a sweetener

### Fermentation

Combine one bottle rum with a handful of apples in a cauldron filled with water over a lit fire and let brew for around three minutes. At this point the cauldron should change to a beige hue and should have sweet white foam and the mixture will be ready to bottle.

Decant the cocktail mixture into three glass bottles at this point the cocktail is ready to drink.

### Drink Information

There are no known positive effects other than the customers' enjoyment of this beverage. This drink is a delicious cocktail made with fruit and fine rum

When brewed correctly This drink will have a cream tan colouration and the sweet taste of citrus. This cocktail has an alcohol rating of I.
